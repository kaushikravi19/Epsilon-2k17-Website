Where creativity meets entrepreneurship. The comfort of our future is in your able hands.
The Epsilon Ideathon is the flagship event of the fest. Our motto for this event is lateral thinking.
We make it true to the De Bono philosophy of attacking
a problem from all angles; instead of bringing up completely new ideas, we encourage ideas
that have died out through the ages and make people expose their potential in the current day.
This idea is what will be an integral part of their pitch. True to our theme, 'A Warp Through Time',
our aim is to bring about a semi-renaissance of ideas where potentials of the past are exposed in the
present and the participants conceive a presentation to do so.
Please note that all participants need to bring their own laptops for the same.
