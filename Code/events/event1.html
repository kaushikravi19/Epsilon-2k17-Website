AIM: To successfully build the specified circuits, in the shortest possible time,
given all required components and sufficient basic theory.
MATERIALS REQUIRED:
1. A team of 2.
2. Logical skills and a love for decoding codes and puzzles.
3. Since electrical components and basic theory will be provided,
arrive equipped with curiosity, enthusiasm and a smidge of information
about the working of radios!
PROCEDURE: The event consists of 2 rounds. The preliminary round will consist of a
written test having logical and analytical questions along with questions
related to basic electronics. Participants will also get to use simple
electronic and measurement devices for a few questions to prove their versatility
and prowess. The qualifying teams will then progress to the Final Round which
will be kept a secret for a little while longer, but it is a guarantee that it
will be a complete thriller from start to finish!
RESULT: Winners: Nobody knows yet, but it could be you!
TAKE-AWAY: A ton of fun and valuable skills! So hurry and register
for this all new, one of a kind experience. The clock is ticking!
