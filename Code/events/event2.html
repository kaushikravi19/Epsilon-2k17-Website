“The mind is not a vessel to be filled, but a fire to be kindled.” –Plutarch.
And what better way to fire up your mind than a mathelogical and scientific
quiz based on a wide array of topics and fields? Make your team of 2
and get ready to spark your curiosity and put all your wits to
test in one of the centrefold events of Epsilon 2017. The
prelims will consist of a written test with a good mix of questions,
ranging from the core principles of science and technology, to tricky
yet solvable brainteasers and a bit of trivia thrown in for good measure,
for the selection of 6 of the sharpest teams to enter the Final Round.
The Final Round will be a superset of 6 rounds, with each of the rounds
containing a myriad of questions based on various audio and visuals, puzzles,
general knowledge and a lot more. Basically, inQUIZitive has got all the makings
of a super fun and brain churning quiz, just the way it should be!
So what are you waiting for? Sign up and come armed and
fireproofed, we're taking the heat up a notch with this one!
