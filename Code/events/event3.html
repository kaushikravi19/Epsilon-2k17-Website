The Earth is in ruins, destroyed by the confluence of global warming,
climate change and a rapid chain of natural disasters. You've fought
tooth and nail to stay alive, but now you must face the final challenge.
It is time to leave this planet behind you and to pull civilisation back from the
brink of extinction. Given this scenario, and the choice of two alternate planets,
debate your way to paradise, as you see it. Present the best solution for
the survival of mankind. The team with the most convincing
arguments and the best style of expression will progress to the further rounds.
Preliminaries will be held based on common science-based controversies/dilemmas.
A life-or-death situation lies ahead, the rest is up to you.

Rules And Regulations

1.Individual event
2.Consists of two rounds - the preliminary and final rounds
3.Preliminary round will be a debate, for or against a randomly allotted proposition. The propositions will be based on common scientific issues
4.Speaker time for preliminary round: 3 minutes per speaker, no rebuttals
5.Participants proceeding to the final round will be given a situation based on the theme "Warp Through Time"
6.This situation will provide the proposition to the speaker, and he/she will need to clarify their stance to the Judges
7.Speaker time for final round: 6 minutes, inclusive of rebuttals
8.Judges' decision is final.
