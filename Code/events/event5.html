Do you have a passion for racing and crave the adrenaline rush that
comes with it? Do you love tinkering and the satisfaction that comes
with building things from scratch? Inspired by the RedBull Annual
Soap Box Race, participate in our very own Soap Box Racing which
tests your creativity, smarts and knowledge of automobiles and their industry.
Comprising of 2 rounds, the first is an elimination round; a
written round that’s all about testing your left brain by
working through puzzles and brain-teasers. Riddle your
way to the second round: the Final Lap, where your
team of 5 will be using all your fabrication skills to build
your own soap box racer with the materials provided. The vehicle
must have steering and braking capabilities and will be judged on the
inventiveness and aesthetics of your design, the time taken to
complete the course and overall performance. So go ahead
and sign up to create the Racer of your dreams, fuelled by passion,
ingenuity, enthusiasm and a thirst for competition to be the Champion of them all!
Ready, set, GO!
